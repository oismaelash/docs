  ## Message rate card API

  **New endpoint** [`GET /v1/pricing`](/api/pricing) — the published WhatsApp Business Platform message rate card (list price \+ volume tiers) by **market × currency × category**. Call it with no params for the available markets, currencies and categories, or with `market` \+ `currency` for the rates. Also available as the [`pricing_get_rates`](/api/pricing) MCP tool and in the [interactive playground](/playground/pricing/get-message-rates).
</Update>
