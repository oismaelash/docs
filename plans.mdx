All paid plans include unlimited messages, media support, and managed proxy. Pick the plan that matches your current scale, and upgrade any time from the dashboard.

## Annual Discount

Every paid plan — Básico, Premium, and Enterprise — is available with annual billing at a **20% discount** compared to the monthly rate. To switch to annual billing, go to **Dashboard → Settings → Billing** and select the annual option for your current plan. The discount is applied immediately and your billing cycle restarts from the switch date.

## Free Plan

The Free plan lets you connect one number using Pilot Status's own managed number, send up to 200 messages in total, and access message logs and webhooks — no credit card required. It is intentionally designed as a sandbox: enough to integrate the API, fire real webhook events, and validate your system end-to-end before you commit to a paid subscription.

<Tip>
  Start on the Free plan to connect your first number and test the API before committing to a paid plan.
</Tip>

Keep in mind that the Free plan does not include media messages, managed proxy, or the ability to connect your own phone number. When you are ready to go to production, upgrading to Básico unlocks all of those features.

## Básico

Básico is the entry-level paid plan for teams that are ready to go live. For R$ 99,90/mo you get three connected numbers, unlimited messages, your own numbers, managed proxy, and full media support (images, videos, and documents). Live Chat is also included, so your team can handle conversations directly from the dashboard.

## Premium

Premium adds the official **Meta Cloud API** integration and **Coexistence mode** to everything in Básico, across up to eight numbers. Use Premium when you need Meta-approved message templates for marketing or transactional flows, or when you need to run the official and unofficial APIs side by side on the same number.

## Enterprise

Enterprise extends Premium to 13 numbers and adds an **eSIM virtual number** — a dedicated WhatsApp-ready number provisioned and managed entirely by Pilot Status, with no physical SIM required. This is the right choice for large-scale deployments that need maximum number capacity and a fully managed telephony layer.
